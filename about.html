About this Website

About

No ads. No trackers. No javascript. Just Recipes.

The premise is simple:
"A recipe site with nothing but recipes and minimal but aesthetic css."

A website doesn't cost a lot of money to keep running. For example, wikipedia has been running for years without ads, only asking for small donations every once in a while.

The way I intend for the recipes to be submitted and curated is much different from wikipedia or a blog. To keep the site as Javascript-free and as light as possible, I also have no intention of adding user acounts in the future. To submit a recipe you can do so either via email or via a pull request on github.
