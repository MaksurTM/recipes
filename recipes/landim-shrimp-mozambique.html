Landim Shrimp - Mozambique

Ingredients

  • 1kg of Shrimp
  • 4 Onions
  • Salt and piri-piri
  • Half dl. of olive oil

Directions

  1. After washing the shrimp, whole and with bark, order them in layers with onion slices, salt and piri-piri.
  2. Water it with olive oil and bake on a low heat.
  3. After baked serve only the shrimp.

Source

“Cozinha Regional Portuguesa”, Maria Odette Cortes Valente [Book]
