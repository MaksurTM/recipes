Algarvian Snails

Directions

  1. Wash small snails very well in various waters until all the viscosity goes away
  2. Put them in cold water and heat it alongside salt and oregano.

Note

They are put in cold water so they exit their shells.

Source

“Cozinha Regional Portuguesa”, Maria Odette Cortes Valente [Book]
