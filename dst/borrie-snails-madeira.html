Borrie Snails - Madeira

Directions

  1. After being well washed, bake them in salt water (sea water if possible) for 15 minutes.
  2. Remove them from their shells, temper them with olive oil, vinagre, garlic cloves (few), bay leaf, pepper and piri-piri.
  3. Put them in jars with the sauce and remove the garlic cloves.
  4. Cover them with oil and cork the jars well.

Source

“Cozinha Regional Portuguesa”, Maria Odette Cortes Valente [Book]
