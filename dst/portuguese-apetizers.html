More than light cookies

Ingredients

  • 200g of margarine
  • 270g of flour
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 Whole egg
  • 1 Egg gem
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Mix everything and mass it lightly. Let it rest for some hours.
  2. Strech the dough with the role and cut circular slices with a cookie-cutter.
  3. Brush them with egg gem so they look golden and put them in the oven with flour.
  4. You use cheese, butter, etc for filling.

Landim Shrimp - Mozambique

Ingredients

  • 1kg of Shrimp
  • 4 Onions
  • Salt and piri-piri
  • Half dl. of olive oil

Directions

  1. After washing the shrimp, whole and with bark, order them in layers with onion slices, salt and piri-piri.
  2. Water it with olive oil and bake on a low heat.
  3. After baked serve only the shrimp.
